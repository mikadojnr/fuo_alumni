Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the FUO Alumni Association, membership, events, and contributions.

All graduates of Federal University Otuoke automatically become members of the Alumni Association. To activate your membership and access all benefits, you need to register on this portal. Simply click on the "Register" button in the navigation menu and follow the instructions to create your account.

Basic membership in the FUO Alumni Association is free. However, we do offer premium membership tiers with additional benefits for a nominal annual fee. Details about premium membership options can be found in the "Membership" section of your dashboard after logging in.

You can update your profile information by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Profile" section in your dashboard. There, you can edit your personal, academic, and professional information, as well as adjust your privacy settings.

You can register for events by visiting the "Events" section of the portal. Click on the event you're interested in to view details, and then click the "Register" button. Some events may have limited capacity or require a registration fee, which will be clearly indicated on the event page.

Yes! We encourage alumni to propose and organize events. Please contact the Alumni Relations Office with your event proposal, including the type of event, target audience, proposed date, and venue. The office will review your proposal and provide guidance on next steps.

Yes, we regularly host virtual events to ensure that our international alumni can participate. These events are clearly marked as "Virtual" in the events calendar. After registering, you'll receive a link to join the event online.

You can contribute to university projects by visiting the "Contributions" section of the portal. Browse the active projects, select one that interests you, and follow the instructions to make a contribution. We accept various payment methods, and you can choose to make your contribution anonymous if you prefer.

Contributions to the university through the alumni association may be tax-deductible. However, tax laws vary, so we recommend consulting with your tax advisor for specific guidance. We provide receipts for all contributions, which you can access in your contribution history.

All contribution funds are managed with transparency and accountability. Funds are allocated specifically to the projects you choose to support. Regular updates on project progress and fund utilization are provided on the project pages. The Alumni Association also publishes an annual financial report that details how all funds were used.

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Login" button, then select "Forgot password?" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Yes, we take data security very seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent. You can also control what information is visible to other alumni through your privacy settings.

If you encounter any technical issues, please contact our support team at support@fuo.edu.ng. Include details about the issue, such as what you were trying to do, any error messages you received, and the device and browser you were using. This will help us resolve the issue more quickly.
