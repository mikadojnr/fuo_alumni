Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my alumni profile?

You can update your profile by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Profile" section in your dashboard.

How can I get involved with the alumni association?

There are many ways to get involved! You can attend events, volunteer for committees, mentor current students, or contribute to projects. Contact us for more information.

Are my contributions tax-deductible?

Contributions to the university through the alumni association may be tax-deductible. Please consult with your tax advisor for specific guidance.

How can I request a transcript or verification of my degree?

Transcript requests and degree verifications are handled by the university's registrar office. Please contact them directly at registrar@fuo.edu.ng.
