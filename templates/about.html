About FUO Alumni Association

Connecting graduates, fostering relationships, and supporting our alma mater.

Our Mission

The Federal University Otuoke Alumni Association is dedicated to fostering a lifelong connection between the university and its graduates, promoting the welfare of the university, and enhancing the professional, intellectual, and social development of our alumni.

We strive to create a vibrant community that supports the university's mission through advocacy, philanthropy, and engagement, while providing valuable resources and opportunities for our alumni to connect, learn, and grow.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic and inclusive alumni association that serves as a bridge between the university and its graduates, fostering a sense of pride, loyalty, and commitment to Federal University Otuoke.

We envision an engaged alumni network that contributes to the university's growth and reputation, while also benefiting from continued connection to their alma mater and fellow graduates.

Our History

2011

University Establishment

Federal University Otuoke was established as part of the federal government's initiative to expand access to tertiary education in Nigeria.

2015

First Graduating Class

The university celebrated its first graduating class, marking a significant milestone in its history.

2016

Alumni Association Formation

The Federal University Otuoke Alumni Association was officially formed to connect graduates and support the university's growth.

2020

Digital Transformation

The association launched its digital platform to better connect alumni across the globe and facilitate engagement with the university.

2023

Alumni Portal Launch

The comprehensive alumni portal was launched, offering enhanced features for networking, event management, and contributions.
